blocks language (EBNF)



Productions (rules)
-------------------

Expr ::= VertExpr Eos .
VertExpr ::= HorizExpr ("|" HorizExpr)* .
HorizExpr ::= PrimaryExpr ("-" PrimaryExpr)* .
PrimaryExpr ::= ParenExpr | RectExpr .
ParenExpr ::= "(" VertExpr ")" .
RectExpr ::= Num "*" Num .



Terminals (tokens)
------------------

Eos, Num, "|", "-", "*", ")", "("
